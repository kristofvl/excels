Why EXCELS?

EXCELS is your gateway to a research-focused master's in AI and Intelligent Sensor Systems. We connect top students from Southeast Asia, China, India, and Sub-Saharan Africa with cutting-edge research and industry opportunities in Germany.

Is EXCELS for You?

If you are a 3rd/4th-year bachelor's student in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or Mechatronics, and you're passionate about AI, sensors, or Edge AI, EXCELS is designed for you!
